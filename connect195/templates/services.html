1.How does the free trial work?
With a free trial, we give you an opportunity to experience working in a co-working space/cafe for an entire day across any of our 150+ locations.

2. do I get on my free day trial?
Your free trial includes access to free wifi, stationery and printing facilities. You also get exclusive discounts, offers and cashbacks on your food bill at our work cafes.

3. How do I register for a free trial?
Just click on the ‘Free Trial Button’ on the website or download the myHQ App and register for a free trial.

4. On my free trial, can I go to any myHQ space?
You can take a trial out of any of our spaces spread across 150+ locations in Delhi NCR. Take your pick between a myriad of choices; co-working or work cafe, get your best fit!

5. Can I avail the ‘Free Trial’ more than once?
Sorry, you can avail a free trial only once. However, our subscription plans are super economical and flexible. So go ahead and pick your best fit without burning a hole in your pocket! :)

6. Do I need to enter my credit card details to avail a free trial?
You don't require to enter your credit card details to book your free day trial. We only need to enter your contact details!

Once you buy a subscription, we add the specified redeemable credits to your account. These can then be used to pay for your food and beverage expense at work cafes or for your visits to coworking spaces

2.How do I pay for my visits to coworking spaces?
Everytime you visit a coworking spaces the Credits will get auto-deducted from your account on a per visit basis

3.Can I use cash/card to pay for my food expenses at coworking spaces?
Yes, you can buy anythingh from coworking cafes.

subscriptions plans are valid for 1 year from the date of purchase

Generally all our spaces are available from 9am - 8pm, Monday through Sunday. However, each co-working space has a different operating time which is mentioned on the detail page on the website and mobile app.
